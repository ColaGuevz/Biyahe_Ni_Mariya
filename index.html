Meet Mariya, Your Local Guide

Mabuhay! I'm Maria, and I'm passionate about sharing the authentic beauty and rich culture of my home, the Philippines. I started "Biyahe ni Mariya" to help travelers like you discover hidden gems, connect with local communities, and create memories that go beyond the usual tourist spots.

Tired of generic tours and tourist traps? I create personalized itineraries focused on ethical practices, immersive cultural experiences, and supporting local businesses. Let's make your Philippine adventure truly special!
